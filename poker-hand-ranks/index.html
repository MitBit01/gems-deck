Gems Deck Poker Hands

The Gems deck has different poker hand ratings than you may be used too. That is because the hands have a different probability, since the cards are different and no card can be played high and low.

List of Hands

The hands are listed in rank order. Hands of the same rank are compared by their highest card. In the case where there are sets of cards (full house, three of a kind, two pair, etc.), compare the high card of the largest set first. If hands differ only in suit, they tie.
